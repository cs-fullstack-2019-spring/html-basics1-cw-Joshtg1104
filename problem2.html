About Me

Joshua, a 27 year old graphic design graduate, born in Mississippi but spent most of his life in Memphis, TN.

Brief History

After attending Raleigh Egypt Elementary for his kindergarten and 1st grade years, he was home schooled until the 4th grade. He attended regular public school until his high school years where he attended a charter school known as Middle College High. After graduating high school he was accepted into University of Memphis where he studied art. After attending U of M for 2 years he transferred to ITT Technical Institute and earned his associate's degree in graphic design.

Now

He is currently working hard on becoming a certified software engineer. He has high hopes for a bright future.
